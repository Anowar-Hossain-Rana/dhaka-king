FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022™

First Come First Served sales period is open. Fans can apply again for tickets for the FIFA World Cup Qatar 2022™ through the First Come First Served sales period of Sales Phase (2) that starts on 5 July 2022 at 11:00 CEST / 12:00 (midday) Doha time and ends on 16 August 2022 at 11:00 CEST / 12:00 (midday) Doha time. During this sales period, tickets will be allocated on a first come, first served basis, and ticket purchases are processed as a real-time transaction, subject to availability. All successfully purchased tickets will be confirmed immediately to the ticket applicants. We advise football fans to place their application as soon as the sales period opens, as tickets will likely sell out quickly..
